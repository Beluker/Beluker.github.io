Rice Addon:

This is version 0.2. Rice spawns naturally in the plains biome, and will endlessly hunt you. They do 1 half heart of damage and have 1 half heart of health.

Installation:

Uncompress the ZIP file and you shall find a Resource Pack and a Behavior Pack. Import both of these into Minecraft and enable them.
